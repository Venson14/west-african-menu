Our Specialties

Efo Riro (Nigerian Spinach Stew)

It is loaded with an assortment of hearty, flavorful proteins—including tender chicken, rich cow skin (ponmo), and honeycomb tripe (shaki)—all swimming in a luscious palm oil base. Paired perfectly with a smooth, fluffy, and perfectly molded mound of pounded yam, this comforting meal offers an unforgettable balance of textures and deep, authentic flavor..

Grilled Whole Fish with Fried Plantains

Savor the ultimate coastal comfort food, this dish features a perfectly grilled whole fish, beautifully charred on the outside while remaining tender and flaky on the inside. It is generously slathered in a vibrant, spicy pepper sauce and garnished with a colorful confetti of fresh bell peppers..

Goat Meat Pepper Soup

Turn up the heat with a comforting classic, this is a light, incredibly aromatic, and fiery broth infused with a deeply complex blend of traditional West African spices.
